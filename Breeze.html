Within the area of the Atlantic Ocean known as the Bermuda Triangle lies a mysterious island. With a radianite mine constructed beneath, a cargo ship beached and in pieces on its shores, and a teleporter operating with unintended functionality, there's much to uncover at this tropical paradise that's of strategic importance to both its homeworld and invaders from Omega.

Breeze is also one of the later maps to valorant coming out in April 27th 2021. It also has only two sites and uses ropes around the map to get to higher areas. The main thing for this map is probably the shaft on A site where you can go from the top of A to middle. This shaft has a small door that automatically opens when you stand on it but it also makes a lot of noise.
