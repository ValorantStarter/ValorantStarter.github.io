Brimstone


  • His C ability allows him to instantly toss a stim beacon that creates a radius for teammates. When you are in the radius or pass the radius you gain multiple buffs. It lasts 12 seconds and you gain equip,reload,recovery speed and increased fire rate.

  • His Q ability equips an incendiary launcher that once fired on the ground with detonate in a radius of fire. This ability lasts for 7 seconds and for every second you are in the fire you take 60 damage. The ability can be shot for a long range or short range.

  • His E ability shows him a screen of the entire map where he can select certain areas to drop down a smoke. He is allowed to play 3 smokes at a time and you slowly regain them over time, each smoke will last for 19 seconds each.

  • His X ultimate shows a screen similar to his E ability except he is only able to select one radius. Once he has selected an area he will launch a strike laser that deal 120 damage in a second. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
