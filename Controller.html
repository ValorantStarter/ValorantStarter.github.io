Astra


  • Her C ability allows you to activate the star making a radius on the ground. This ability will allow them to pull in the enemy onto the middle. After 2.75 seconds anyone inside the abilility will become vulnerable.

  • Her Q ability allows her to activate another star making a radius on the ground. This ability will concuss the enemies. After 1.25 seconds the ability will activate and it will be harder to shoot and move.

  • Her E ability has another star ability that places down a smoke on the ground. This will be able to block the view of an enemy from a certain area. This ability will last 14 seconds and 1.5 seconds to return.

  • Her X ultimate allows her to place a giant wall that is impenetrable. While the wall is up you won't be able to hear anything from the other side for 21 seconds. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Brimstone


  • His C ability allows him to instantly toss a stim beacon that creates a radius for teammates. When you are in the radius or pass the radius you gain multiple buffs. It lasts 12 seconds and you gain equip,reload,recovery speed and increased fire rate.

  • His Q ability equips an incendiary launcher that once fired on the ground with detonate in a radius of fire. This ability lasts for 7 seconds and for every second you are in the fire you take 60 damage. The ability can be shot for a long range or short range.

  • His E ability shows him a screen of the entire map where he can select certain areas to drop down a smoke. He is allowed to play 3 smokes at a time and you slowly regain them over time, each smoke will last for 19 seconds each.

  • His X ultimate shows a screen similar to his E ability except he is only able to select one radius. Once he has selected an area he will launch a strike laser that deal 120 damage in a second. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Harbor


  • His C ability allows him to deploy a giant wall that moves the direction it is facing. You are able to shoot through it and can stop it from moving with C. Anyone who goes through the ability will be 30% slower for 2 seconds.

  • His Q ability will equip a sphere of water that can be thrown and turn into a circle with a sheild around it. The shield has 500 hp and the ability lasts for 15 seconds, once the shield is gone anyone will be able to shoot through.

  • His E ability another wall ability except it can be controlled using your mouse. If you hold fire and look around the wall will go in that direction. This wall will last for 15 seconds and if you walk through it you have gain 30% slowness.

  • His X ultimate allows him to summon a giant radius of water moving wherever you are faced. Anyone inside will be able to get stunned if hit by lightning for 2 seconds. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Omen



  • Her Q ability throws down a blast pack which has a timer of 5 seconds before it explodes. You can also manually explode the pack which boosts you in any direction making movement easier. It also can push away enemies and do minimal damage.

  • Her E ability equips a grenade that can be thrown far with left click and you can also lob for shorter distance with the right click. After the grenade explodes the first time it seperates into a cluster dealing extra damage with the extra grenades.

  • Her X ultimate equips a rocket launcher with a 10 second timer. Once the launcher is fired it pushes her back and sends the rocket directly where you aim and has a small radius. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Reyna


  • Her C ability equips a purple eye that can be thrown out in the air or wherever you aim. If an enemy has the eye on their screen it temporarily blinds them making it harder to see. It lasts for 1.6 seconds but the range of the blind is unlimited.

  • Her Q ability gives her the chance to harvest health from the enemies she has damaged/killed. But you can only use it within 3 seconds of the kill and it gives you 100 hp maxing at 150. The extra health you get past 100 decays after 30 seconds.

  • Her E ability gives her a second option when getting damage or a kill. It allows her to become almost invinsible and makes her invinsible for 2 seconds. She isn't allowed to fire at enemies when in this form but can get out of situations quickly.

  • Her X ultimate instantly increases her fire rate and reload speed and has infinite charges for her 2 kill abilites. When using her E ability she becomes fully invisible and increases speed. This takes 6 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
