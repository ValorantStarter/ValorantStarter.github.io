Cypher


  • His C ability equips a tripwire he can place on a connecting surface. This will also go invisible unless an enemy is near. Once triggered the enemy will be trapped and will be stunned in 3 seconds. It can also be destroyed but it deals 20 damage.

  • His Q ability makes him toss a disk that can be activated by cypher. Once activated it will creates a zone that acts like a smoke and blocks vision. Any enemy that goes through it will make a noise to alert anyone near by.

  • His E ability equips a camera that he can place that will be invisible unless active. Cypher can use his cam wherever as long as it isn't broken. You are able to shoot an enemy with a tracking dart through the camera to mark them unless they take it out.

  • His X ultimate is able to throw his hat at a dead enemy body that will mark all the enemies. His ultimate marks the enemies 2 times and shows a highlight of them on the map or through walls. This takes 6 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
