Phoenix


  • His C ability is a fire wall which can both heal him and damage opponents/teamates. It also blocks line of sight but can still be shot through. You can control where it goes by holding the left mouse button and aiming it around.

  • His Q ability allows him to throw a flash ball blinding anyone who looks at it. It does make a curve when throwing making it easier to clear corner. He can throw it left or right depending the mouse button hit.

  • His E ability is a pool of fire that he can throw to heal and damage opponents or his team. After every 2 kills he gets after using it he gets back the ability. He can throw it far or short and close to him.

  • His X ultimate creates a clone of himself which lasts 10 seconds or upon death. After that time is up he goes back where he used his ultimate giving him another life. This takes 6 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Jett


  • Her C ability instantly throws out a projectile that can be controlled by holding C and moving your mouse. Once it is placed down it will creat a ball of smoke that covers a certain area. This ability lasts for 4.5 seconds once placed.

  • Her Q ability instantly throws her up in the air making it easier to get to certain areas. This ability combined with her passive will make it easier to see enemies while in the air. Her passive is used in the air while holding the space bar.

  • Her E ability allows her to start a timer on when she can dash that lasts 12 seconds. In this time you are able to use the dash ability to get out of a hard situation quicker. You can go in any direction depending where you are walking.

  • Her X ultimate pulls out 5 knives and with every kill you get all your knives back. The left click shoots the knives singularly while the right click shoots all at once for close range. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Neon


  • Her C ability creates 2 energy walls that blocks both sides making it easy to get through the objective without having to look both ways. The wall travels for a short distance or until it hits a wall. This ability lasts for 6 seconds after being fully placed.

  • Her Q ability instantly shoots out an energy bolt that bounces once of the wall the first bolt hit. After hitting the surface it creates a small circle and stunning anyone in the circle for 3 seconds. When stunned it makes people shoot and move slower.

  • Her E ability increases her speed to get to places quicker but it drains her energy which recharges everytime it isn't used. If you right click with the ability it allows her to slide and every 2 kills it recharges the ability allowing you to slide again.

  • Her X ultimate allows her to shoot a laser through her finger tips dealing a lot of damage. Everytime she gets a kill the energy resets since it starts to drain whenever using the ability. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Raze


  • Her C ability lets her summon a boom bot that can be placed down and when there is an enemy in it sight it runs to it and explodes. Once it hits a wall it bounces in the opposite direction and lasts for 10 seconds or until it explodes or gets broken.

  • Her Q ability throws down a blast pack which has a timer of 5 seconds before it explodes. You can also manually explode the pack which boosts you in any direction making movement easier. It also can push away enemies and do minimal damage.

  • Her E ability equips a grenade that can be thrown far with left click and you can also lob for shorter distance with the right click. After the grenade explodes the first time it seperates into a cluster dealing extra damage with the extra grenades.

  • Her X ultimate equips a rocket launcher with a 10 second timer. Once the launcher is fired it pushes her back and sends the rocket directly where you aim and has a small radius. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Reyna


  • Her C ability equips a purple eye that can be thrown out in the air or wherever you aim. If an enemy has the eye on their screen it temporarily blinds them making it harder to see. It lasts for 1.6 seconds but the range of the blind is unlimited.

  • Her Q ability gives her the chance to harvest health from the enemies she has damaged/killed. But you can only use it within 3 seconds of the kill and it gives you 100 hp maxing at 150. The extra health you get past 100 decays after 30 seconds.

  • Her E ability gives her a second option when getting damage or a kill. It allows her to become almost invinsible and makes her invinsible for 2 seconds. She isn't allowed to fire at enemies when in this form but can get out of situations quickly.

  • Her X ultimate instantly increases her fire rate and reload speed and has infinite charges for her 2 kill abilites. When using her E ability she becomes fully invisible and increases speed. This takes 6 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Yoru


  • His C ability equips a copy of himself that can be placed on the ground and activated later or placed automatically. This decoy looks identical to yoru but only moves in one direction and once destroyed it blinds anyone looking in its direction.

  • His Q ability lets him equip a blind that can ricochet of a surface one time before activating. If can be thrown from a short or far distance making it easier to cover more ground. This ability blinds anyone looking at it for 1.6 seconds.

  • His E ability lets him equip a teleport that can be placed where you stand or can be sent out into the direction you look at. Once placed you may be able to activate it and go to the same location of the ability making it easier to move around.

  • His X ultimate equips a mask that lets him travel between dimensions making him invisible. He can walk around the map but can't shoot or be shot, the ultimate lasts 12 seconds. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
