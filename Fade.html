Fade


  • Her C abiility summons a prowler that can be controlled by holding left clicking and moving your mouse. It leaves behind a trail and once an enemy is spotted it targets it automatically. The prowler can be shot and only lasts for 2.5 seconds.

  • Her Q ability equips a ball that can be thrown and upon impact will grab onto enemies in the radius. While being stuck you won't be able to move out of it and will be stuck while being deafened. You will also lose 75 health for 4.5 seconds.

  • Her E ability allows her to throw a ball that once landed will scan enemies in it's sight. It will also place down a trail that leads to the marked enemy that lasts for 12 seconds. If it does not get shot or destroyed it will last for 2 seconds.

  • Her X ultimate shoots out a wave that slowly pushes forward. If any enemy is caught in that wave then they'll be marked by a trail. They will also be weak and deafened for 12 seconds. This takes 8 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
