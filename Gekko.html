Gekko


  • His C ability equips a creature that acts like a grenade. You can throw far with left click and throw it closer with right. Once they land it will make a radius on the floor with a 3 second timer. When the timer is up the radius will explode dealing 150 damage if in the middle of it.

  • His Q ability equips a creature that will follow a direct path and will stun anyone in there path. However if you have the spike and right click to on a place to plant he will do it for you. If you are on defense this creature will also be able to defuse, but he can be destroyed.

  • His E ability equips another creature that can blind enemies. He can be thrown in any direction and will blind enemies for 1 second. This ability however can't blind teammates and is the only blind that won't do that so far. When you throw the ability it will speed up before slowing down.

  • His X ultimate equips a creature can you can control. You can stay in this ultimate for 10 seconds or until you hit an enemy. When you hit an enemy they will be detained for 6 seconds and not allowed to do anything. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
