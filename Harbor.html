Harbor


  • His C ability allows him to deploy a giant wall that moves the direction it is facing. You are able to shoot through it and can stop it from moving with C. Anyone who goes through the ability will be 30% slower for 2 seconds.

  • His Q ability will equip a sphere of water that can be thrown and turn into a circle with a sheild around it. The shield has 500 hp and the ability lasts for 15 seconds, once the shield is gone anyone will be able to shoot through.

  • His E ability another wall ability except it can be controlled using your mouse. If you hold fire and look around the wall will go in that direction. This wall will last for 15 seconds and if you walk through it you have gain 30% slowness.

  • His X ultimate allows him to summon a giant radius of water moving wherever you are faced. Anyone inside will be able to get stunned if hit by lightning for 2 seconds. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
