Fade


  • Her C abiility summons a prowler that can be controlled by holding left clicking and moving your mouse. It leaves behind a trail and once an enemy is spotted it targets it automatically. The prowler can be shot and only lasts for 2.5 seconds.

  • Her Q ability equips a ball that can be thrown and upon impact will grab onto enemies in the radius. While being stuck you won't be able to move out of it and will be stuck while being deafened. You will also lose 75 health for 4.5 seconds.

  • Her E ability allows her to throw a ball that once landed will scan enemies in it's sight. It will also place down a trail that leads to the marked enemy that lasts for 12 seconds. If it does not get shot or destroyed it will last for 2 seconds.

  • Her X ultimate shoots out a wave that slowly pushes forward. If any enemy is caught in that wave then they'll be marked by a trail. They will also be weak and deafened for 12 seconds. This takes 8 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

KAY/O


  • His C ability equips a that can be thrown with left click for far range or right click for close. Once it lands it sends three blasts in the area and if you get hit by a blast it deals 25-60 damage. After 4 seconds have past the grenade will dissapear.

  • His Q ability equips a flash grenade that can be thrown far or close range. It can be bounced of walls but it won't bounce far off. If you throw it short you will be blinded for 1.25 seconds and if you throw it regular it will last 2.25 seconds.

  • His E ability lets him equip a throwing knife that will activate when it hits a surface. It will create a radius and get rid of the abilities of people in the radius. You are able to shoot down the knife once it hits the surface and it doesn't deal damage.

  • His X ultimate gives him a radius around him that gets rid of abilites. He will also get a faster fire rate and reload speed. If he is knocked down in his ultimate he will be able to get revived. This takes 8 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Skye


  • Her C ability equips a heal orb that when fired heals allies at a certain radius. It can keep being used until the energy depletes and skye cannot heal herself. It gives 20 heals per second and has a 100 energy while healing multiple people at a time.

  • Her Q ability equips a dog that she can control and see through. This will allow to stun enemies on contact and can leap forward. Her dog only lasts 6 seconds and can be shot by the enemy to break and Skye can be shot while controlling her dog.

  • Her E ability equips a bird and can be thrown and controlled following your crosshair. Once activated it will blind everyone that is looking at it's general direction. This can also be shot down but skye is allowed to move while controlling the bird.

  • Her X ultimate equips 3 orbs that will start tracking down different enemies. This ultimate can be shot while chasing the enemies. If it hits an enemy they will be nearsighted for 5 seconds. This takes 7 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Sova


  • His C ability equips an owl drone that can be controlled and move around for 7 seconds. This will allow you to scan enemies when hit with a dart but the drone can be destroyed. Sova can still be damaged while he is controlling the drone.

  • His Q ability lets him use a shock dart that can be bounced 1 or 2 times. Once making its finally landing it will produce a small radius that will damage enemies and allies. You can charge the bow up to 100% to change the distance it goes.

  • His E ability lets him use a recon dart that can be bounced 1 or 2 times. This will start to scan the area it lands in and highlights enemies that were scanned. This dart can be destroyed and scans the surrounding are for 3.2 seconds.

  • His X ultimate equips an electric charged bow that has wall piercing arrows that deliver a radius when shot. This ultimate has 3 shots and deals 80 damage each and can hit allies. This takes 8 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.

Breach


  • His C ability fire a charge that slowly shoots through a wall 3 times. While it fires there is a damage radius that deals damage to enemies and allies. It lasts for 2.2 seconds and can be shot from a far distance and also slows people in the radius.

  • His Q ability equips a flash that can go through walls to blind enemies on the other side. This flash lasts for 2 seconds if an enemy or teammate looks in its direction. His flash comes with 2 charges and it only takes 0.5 seconds for it to activate once deployed.

  • His E ability will allow him to stun enemies in a lined up radius. Charging up the ability before using it makes it go farther distances. The stun can go through walls and it lasts 3.5 seconds, keep in mind this will also stun your team mates.

  • His X ultimate will shoot out a wave of seismic shocks that will launch team mates and enemies in the air. This covers a large area and only goes straight and the stun lasts for 6 seconds. This takes 8 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
