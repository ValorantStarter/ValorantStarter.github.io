KAY/O


  • His C ability equips a that can be thrown with left click for far range or right click for close. Once it lands it sends three blasts in the area and if you get hit by a blast it deals 25-60 damage. After 4 seconds have past the grenade will dissapear.

  • His Q ability equips a flash grenade that can be thrown far or close range. It can be bounced of walls but it won't bounce far off. If you throw it short you will be blinded for 1.25 seconds and if you throw it regular it will last 2.25 seconds.

  • His E ability lets him equip a throwing knife that will activate when it hits a surface. It will create a radius and get rid of the abilities of people in the radius. You are able to shoot down the knife once it hits the surface and it doesn't deal damage.

  • His X ultimate gives him a radius around him that gets rid of abilites. He will also get a faster fire rate and reload speed. If he is knocked down in his ultimate he will be able to get revived. This takes 8 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
