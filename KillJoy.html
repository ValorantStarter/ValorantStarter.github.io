KillJoy


  • Her C ability equips a swarm grenade that you left click to throw far and right click for a closer throw. Once it's placed it will appear invisible to enemies unless close to it. Once activated a radius of nanobots on the ground will appear damaging anyone in it.

  • Her Q ability equips an alarmbot that is placed on the ground and turns invisible. When an enemy is near, it activates and runs towards them to explode. When the alarm bot explodes it makes the enemy vulnerable. You can recall the alarmbot by holding the Q key.

  • Her E ability equips a turret that is placed on flat surfaces. If you want to turn the bot just right click before placing. Once placed it will move in a 180 degree cone looking for enemies to shoot. You can recall the bot by holding the E key.

  • Her X ultimate equips a beacon that can be placed on the ground to create a radius. If you are in the radius for 13 seconds you will be detained. You can only move when detained and it lasts for 8 seconds. This takes 8 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
