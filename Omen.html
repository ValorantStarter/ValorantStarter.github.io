Omen


  • His C ability allows him to select a close area in his view to teleport to. While teleporting you are invincible but once finished you can be shot again. When you teleport you make a noise and anyone around will be able to tell you teleported to a different area.

  • His Q ability equips a blind that can be thrown out in any direction for a short distance it can also go through walls. If you are hit with the blind then you will be afflicted with paranoia. Paranoia makes the player who got hit nearsighted and deafened for 2 seconds.

  • His E ability takes him to another screen where he can see the whole map to be able to place his smokes. He can change his view with the reload button making him see the regular map. He gets 2 smokes and they both last for 15 seconds before dissapearing.

  • His X ultimate will allows him to see the whole map and can select any area. Once he has selected an area he will be able to teleport to it. He takes 4 seconds to fully teleport and in that time he can cancel it or it can also be shot by enemies.
