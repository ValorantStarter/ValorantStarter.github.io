Reyna


  • Her C ability equips a purple eye that can be thrown out in the air or wherever you aim. If an enemy has the eye on their screen it temporarily blinds them making it harder to see. It lasts for 1.6 seconds but the range of the blind is unlimited.

  • Her Q ability gives her the chance to harvest health from the enemies she has damaged/killed. But you can only use it within 3 seconds of the kill and it gives you 100 hp maxing at 150. The extra health you get past 100 decays after 30 seconds.

  • Her E ability gives her a second option when getting damage or a kill. It allows her to become almost invinsible and makes her invinsible for 2 seconds. She isn't allowed to fire at enemies when in this form but can get out of situations quickly.

  • Her X ultimate instantly increases her fire rate and reload speed and has infinite charges for her 2 kill abilites. When using her E ability she becomes fully invisible and increases speed. This takes 6 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
