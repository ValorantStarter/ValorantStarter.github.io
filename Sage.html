Sage


  • Her C ability equips an orb that is able to create a wall. If you right click it will rotate the wall to place it somewhere else. Once it is places it gets 800 health, the wall lasts 40 seconds unless destroyed.

  • Her Q ability equips another orb that can be through. Once it hits the ground it realeases a field that players can walk on. If you walk on the crystals you will become 50% slower. This lasts for 7 seconds.

  • Her E ability equips an orb that can be used to heal teammates or herself. If you heal a teammate it heals them for 100 hp in 5 seconds. If you heal yourself, you only heal for 30 hp in 10 seconds.

  • Her X ultimate allows her to revive a teammate when they die. They will take 3 seconds to fully revive and they only get to have 100 hp. This takes 8 points which you get with kills,planting/defusing or orbs.
